It was the eighth time the US had struck Houthi targets, and the second time the UK was involved. On Friday, the Marlin Luanda - a British-linked oil tanker in the Gulf of Aden - was set on fire for several hours after being hit by a missile fired by the Houthis.The fire was extinguished with no crew injured after French, Indian and US naval ships provided assistance to the vessel.The Houthis said it targeted the vessel in response to "American-British aggression against our country".The Houthis have targeted foreign ships in the area since the start of the Israel-Hamas war.
